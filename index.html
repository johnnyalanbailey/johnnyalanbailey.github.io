Welcome to JABS

JABS (Just About Blood Sugar) provides personalised support, education, and guidance to help you manage your blood sugars and live well with diabetes.

JABS does not offer any medical or prescriptive advice. Under UK legislation, this advice can only be provided by a qualified healthcare professional. JABS is an educational service aimed at empowering individuals with knowledge and skills to manage their diabetes effectively.

Our Services

  • One-on-one diabetes coaching sessions, virtually or in person
  • Education on blood sugar management
  • Support for lifestyle changes
  • Real insight from a diabetic with a sustained HbA1c of 5.1%

About Us

Here at JABS, I believe in education above all else. Afterall, how can you be expected to achieve great sugars if you are unaware of the many influences on your blood sugars - from the 5 blood-glucose increasing hormones to the interactions of different food molecules on your digestive system and endocrine system? Yes, it's complicated, but that's what I do best.

Currently teamed by one diabetes coach, Johnny Bailey, a type one diabetic of over 5 years with a sustained HbA1c of less than 5.6% throughout this timespan, JABS coaching is formulated from the experiences of real diabetics. Gone are the days where you are left in the dark by your diabetes teams, JABS is here to help and here to guide diabetics through the mostly unknown surprises of living with diabetes.

Johnny has a passion for helping others and has been involved in diabetes education for several years. He understands the challenges of living with diabetes and is dedicated to providing the support you need.

Whether you are newly diagnosed or have been living with diabetes for years, JABS is here to help you navigate your journey with confidence and knowledge.
