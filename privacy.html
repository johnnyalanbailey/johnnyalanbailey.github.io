Privacy Policy

At JABS (Just About Blood Sugars), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in accordance with UK law, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any details you provide when booking a consultation or contacting us. We only collect data that is necessary for providing our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, and provide diabetes coaching services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except where required by law.

Special Category Data - reasons for processing:

As Just About Blood Sugar asks for your HbA1c and other healthcare information, this means the data you provide sits in a 'special category' under UK GDPR. Legally, Just About Blood Sugar is therefore required to state our condition for processing this data:

  • Informed analysis of diabetes management for greater specificity during coaching sessions.
  • Explicit consent is the condition met under Article 9(2)(a) of the UK GDPR Act, taken during booking of initial consultation and after purchase of the coaching and WhatsApp services we offer.
    • The reason for processing special category data is the same across all of our legal bases for processing.

CONSENT

Here at Just About Blood Sugar, we are passionate about optional consent. However, for us to carry out our services effectively, we require certain information be provided to us before booking or attending an appointment:

  • HbA1c (optional), Date of diagnosis, personal details including name, email, phone number (optional), date of birth, medications, type of diabetes, additional information, current struggles with diabetes, previous techniques tried, goals from coaching, etc.

Where certain data is not required for us to complete our services and contract with you, such as contacting your diabetes team (an optional addition to your coaching with us), we request explicit consent and inform you that this data is not necessary:

  • Diabetes team contact details. When explicit consent is given severally to the other data processing requirements of our contract with you, we will contact your healthcare team using these details provided. By giving explicit consent, you authorise the sharing of your current health details and coaching appointment notes â€“ which are always shared with you before being sent to your team, to allow for an opt-out. You may withdraw consent without detriment at any time.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. All information is stored securely and only accessible to authorised personnel. Your data is stored securely in our systems and will be deleted upon request.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

  • You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • You can request deletion of your data where appropriate.
  • You can object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • You have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at diabetescoaching@justaboutbloodsugar.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and you will be notified via email if you have provided us with your contact details.
